Welcome to Maap

This application gives user the opportunity to search for events and other activities. Records will be imported and categorized via connections to excisting social network and portals. Focus is the integration of external sources and extended search, so that users can filter and search the data by different factors, especially current position and by map. This prototype shows the general functionality and will evaluate eventual further development of the project.
